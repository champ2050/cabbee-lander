Privacy Policy

Who we are
Cabbee is a private cab service based in Mumbai. This page is our website used specifically for recruiting drivers. We do not have an app.

What information we collect
When you call us to apply for the driver job, we take your name and phone number. That is the only personal information we collect directly through this page. We do not collect your email, your address, or any payment details through this website.

How we use your information
Your phone number is used only to discuss the driver vacancy, schedule a meeting, or coordinate document verification. We do not call you for any other reason. We do not send marketing messages.

Google Ads and cookies
This website uses Google Ads to show our recruitment ads to drivers. Google uses cookies (small files saved on your device) to do this. A cookie may be placed on your device when you visit this page, so that Google knows your visit happened. This helps us understand whether our ads are working.

Google may also use this information to show you ads from us on other websites you visit. If you do not want to see personalised ads from Google, you can change your settings at adssettings.google.com.

You can also read Google's own privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Conversion tracking
When you tap the call button on this page, we record that the call happened. We use this information only to understand how many people who saw our ad went on to call us. This data is processed through Google's conversion tracking system. We do not store your personal details as part of this.

How long data is kept
Your phone number stays with us only as long as needed to fill the vacancy. After that it is not used unless you are hired. Google keeps its own tracking data as per its own privacy policy linked above.

Sharing your data
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. The only third party involved is Google, which processes anonymised tracking data as described above.

Your rights
You can ask us to delete your phone number from our records at any time. Just call or message us and we will remove it.

Questions
If you have any question about how we handle your information, call us on the number shown on this page and we will be happy to explain.
