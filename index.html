Privacy Policy

Who we are
Cabbee is a private outstation cab service based in Mumbai. This page is our only website. We do not have an app.

What information we collect
When you call us to book a trip, we take your name and phone number. That is the only personal information we collect directly. We do not collect your email, your address, or any payment details through this website.

How we use your information
Your phone number is used only to confirm your booking, send you your driver's details before the trip, and coordinate your pickup. We do not call you for any other reason. We do not send marketing messages.

Google Ads and cookies
This website uses Google Ads to show our ads to people who may be looking for outstation cabs. Google uses cookies (small files saved on your device) to do this. A cookie may be placed on your device when you visit this page, so that Google knows your visit happened. This helps us understand whether our ads are working.

Google may also use this information to show you ads from us on other websites you visit. If you do not want to see personalised ads from Google, you can change your settings at adssettings.google.com.

You can also read Google's own privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Conversion tracking
When you tap the call button on this page, we record that the call happened. We use this information only to understand how many people who saw our ad went on to call us. This data is processed through Google's conversion tracking system. We do not store your personal details as part of this.

How long data is kept
Your phone number stays with us only as long as needed to complete your trip. After that it is not used. Google keeps its own tracking data as per its own privacy policy linked above.

Sharing your data
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. The only third party involved is Google, which processes anonymised tracking data as described above.

Your rights
You can ask us to delete your phone number from our records at any time. Just call or message us and we will remove it.

Questions
If you have any question about how we handle your information, call us on the number shown on this page and we will be happy to explain.

Terms & Conditions

The service
Cabbee is a single-driver, private outstation cab service operating from Mumbai. Trip types are offered based on availability and business discretion, and may include round trips, one-way drops, or custom itineraries. The exact trip type and route are confirmed on the phone call. If a round trip is booked, your driver stays with you for the full trip and brings you back home.

Price
The rate is ₹16 per km for round trips, fixed. One-way or custom trips may be priced differently based on route and availability. This covers AC, fuel both ways, driver charges, roof carrier, and night travel for round trips. The round-trip price is calculated on the total round trip distance. There are no hidden charges in the base rate. The rate shown on this page is the current rate at the time of your visit. Rates may be revised during peak seasons, festivals, or based on demand. Your final rate is confirmed when you call and book. Once confirmed, your rate will not change for that trip.

What you pay on the road
Tolls, state border taxes, and parking charges are not included in the ₹16 rate. These are paid on the road at the real government price. Whatever the toll booth or official counter charges, that is what you pay. We will give you a receipt for every payment made on the road. Nothing is added on top by us.

Booking and advance payment
To confirm your trip, we collect 15% of the total trip cost in advance. This amount is paid before the trip starts, via UPI or bank transfer. Your trip is only confirmed once this advance is received. The remaining 85% is paid after your trip is complete, in cash or UPI.

Cancellation
You can cancel for free if you inform us 6 or more hours before your pickup time. Your advance will be fully returned within 24 hours. If you cancel within 6 hours of your pickup time, the advance amount will not be returned, as the trip has already been prepared for.

Driver and car
You will be told who your driver is and given the car number before your trip day. The car is a Maruti Suzuki Dzire Tour S CNG. If for any genuine emergency the car or driver changes, you will be informed as early as possible and given the choice to cancel with a full refund.

Driver verification and safety
All drivers are Aadhaar-verified and hold a valid commercial driving licence. We run a background check before any driver is assigned a trip. Your driver's name, photo, and phone number are shared with you before the trip day so you know exactly who is picking you up. If you are travelling solo or would like someone at home to have the driver's details, we are happy to share them with a family member or friend as well.

Conduct during the trip
We ask that passengers treat the driver with respect. Any damage caused to the car by passengers will be charged at cost. Smoking is not allowed inside the car.

First booking offer
The ₹200 discount is for first-time customers only. You must mention it when you call to book. It cannot be applied after the booking is confirmed. One discount per customer.

Problems or complaints
If anything goes wrong with your trip, call us directly and we will sort it out. We take complaints seriously. Our goal is for you to have a good trip and come back the next time.

Governing rules
This service operates under the laws of India. Any disputes will be handled in Mumbai.
